#DataAnalytics #BusinessIntelligence #DataViz #ioT


I’m a practiced data analyst and data visualization designer based in Paris, France.


I discovered my passion for data during my MBA at Audencia Business School in Nantes, France. I taught myself how to code using as many courses and resource as I could find. After graduating in 2021, I continued training as a data analyst through online courses, certifications, and independent consulting.


I love data because it combines the precision of analysis with the creativity of design: a perfect meeting of my two worlds as a former industrial engineer and procurement professional, and my personal passion for coding and data visualization. Prior to my graduate studies, I worked with some of the world’s most recognizable brands in manufacturing, operational logistics, and supply chain management. Now, I work with spreadsheets and SQL, and have completed several projects in Python. I have also created several data visualization projects from scratch, using BI tools like Tableau and PowerBI alongside Figma and Adobe Illustrator


This website is my personal page to showcase and share my data work with the larger community. I’m also currently looking for my next step! I am actively searching for full-time and consulting opportunities in the data analysis space.

Please feel free to browse my portfolio, review my various projects, and don’t hesitate to reach out if you’re looking for someone with my skillset!
